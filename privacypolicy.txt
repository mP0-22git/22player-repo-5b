Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Music Player - MP3 & Audio ("the App") is developed by Elysium Group. This privacy policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our App.

The App is open source and the source code can be viewed on our GitHub page.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal information from users. The App operates primarily on your device, and your music library, playlists, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information. These services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google Play Services
Used for core app functionality and distribution through the Google Play Store.
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob
The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve ads, including personalized ads based on your interests. This data may include:
- Device advertising identifier
- IP address (used to estimate general location)
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- App interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
- On Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

For more information, see Google's advertising privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Superwall
The App uses Superwall to display and manage subscription offerings. Superwall collects data to facilitate paywall presentation and to analyze the effectiveness of different subscription screens. This may include device identifiers and information about how you interact with subscription screens. Superwall does not have access to your payment information.
Privacy Policy: https://superwall.com/privacy

Google Play Billing
Subscriptions and purchases are processed through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details such as credit card numbers. Google handles all payment processing and billing. Your purchase history with our App may be visible to us through the Google Play Console for the purpose of providing customer support and honoring your subscription status.
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Last.fm
The App uses the Last.fm API to retrieve artist biographies and album information, including artwork. When you view artist or album details, the App sends the artist and album names to Last.fm's servers to fetch this content. The free version of the App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. If you purchase a subscription or have previously purchased the Pro version, advertisements are not displayed.

Ads shown may be personalized based on your interests, or you may see non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and regional regulations.
